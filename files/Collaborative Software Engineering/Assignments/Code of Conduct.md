## Assignment
---

- Indicate a time-frame/due date for each agreement (if applicable)
- Follow SMART principles
- Update the code of conduct over time if needed

1. **Assignment Description**
2. **Target or ambition level**
3. **Planning**
4. **Communication**
5. **Collaboration outside of the mandatory meetings**
6. **Helping each other**

8. **Decision-making**
9. **Broken agreements**
10. **Problem resolution**
